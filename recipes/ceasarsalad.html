Caesar Salad

Make a scrumptious appetizer with this recipe for Caesar Salad!

The Caesar Salad is a crisp, fresh, and creamy dish that strikes the perfect balance between savory, tangy, and rich flavors. The base consists of crunchy romaine lettuce, providing a refreshing texture that contrasts beautifully with the velvety dressing.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce (about 6 cups, chopped)
  • 1 cup croutons (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese (or more to taste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg yolk (or 1 tbsp mayonnaise for a safer option)
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp anchovy paste (optional but recommended for authentic flavor)
  • ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

    • Steps to make

    1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, egg yolk, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, and anchovy paste.
    2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while whisking continuously to emulsify the dressing until it’s smooth.
    3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    4. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce, then chop or tear it into bite-sized pieces.
    5. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with the Caesar dressing, making sure each leaf is coated.
    6. Add croutons to the dressed lettuce and toss again.
    7. Sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top and give it a final toss to distribute evenly.
    8. Plate the salad immediately, or serve family-style in a large bowl.
