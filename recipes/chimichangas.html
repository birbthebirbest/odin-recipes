Chimichangas

Make a yummy chimichanga using this recipe!

Beef Chimichangas filled with a ground beef/refried bean mixture seasoned with chili powder, cumin, and oregano, rolled up and fried until crispy, and covered with cheese and jalapeno tomato sauce tastes so much better than restaurant chimichangas. They are sure to be a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Beef
  • 1/2 cup of diced onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 8 tortillas
  • Queso
  • Vegetable oil

    • Steps to Make:

    1. Brown ground beef in a nonstick skillet. breaking it apart with a wooden spoon as it cooks. When it is about halfway cooked, add onion.
    2. Once onion is softened and beef is cooked, add garlic, chili powder, and cumin. Stir to mix and cook 30 seconds.
    3. Add 1/2 cup tomato sauce from the 8-ounce can. Save leftover tomato sauce to add to sauce.
    4. Stir to mix and remove from heat.
    5. Place about 1/3 cup filling towards one side of a tortilla. Fold over the side nearest the filling to cover the filling. Fold in the two sides and roll up. Secure with toothpicks. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
    6. Heat about 2 inches of oil to 375 degrees in a Dutch oven (or whatever type of pan you like to fry in).
    7. Fry 2 chimichangas at a time, about 2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
    8. Top chimichangas with Queso
